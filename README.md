## About

Abacus is a project that resolves research problems and delivers high-quality, actionable suggestions to sellers on Korean marketplaces.

- Purpose: Provide data-driven, prioritized recommendations to help sellers improve product listings, pricing, and marketing performance by combining marketplace signals with social media trends and seasonal insights.
- Target users: Online sellers and marketplace managers on Korean open shopping platforms, e-commerce analysts, product teams, and digital marketers.
- Problem solved: Automates and accelerates competitive research and decision-making by turning aggregated research insights into clear, implementable suggestions and prioritized action items.


## Features

- New & Hot Product Tracking
  - Continuously monitor specified categories to detect new listings and identify fast-rising (hot) products so sellers can spot opportunities early.

- Keyword, Trend & Seasonality Tracking
  - Track product listings and market activity by keywords and search trends, and detect seasonal patterns that impact demand and pricing.

- Multi-platform SNS Trend Monitoring
  - Aggregate trend signals from social platforms commonly used in Korea and globally (Google Trends, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Naver Cafe, Naver Blog, etc.) to surface emerging topics, influencer signals, and content-driven demand shifts.

- Aggregation-driven AI Reports
  - Combine marketplace data and SNS signals into concise, prioritized AI-generated reports and suggestions for sellers (e.g., product positioning, title/description improvements, pricing recommendations, promotional timing).

- Actionable Recommendations
  - Deliver clear, ranked tasks sellers can act on immediately, with rationale and confidence scores.
